16  * The MultiResultProcessor processes an incoming envelope with a given MultiResultGraphstage
17  * with a given Flow[FlowEnvelope, FlowEnvelope]. It will collect the results from the sub flow

19  *
20  * Pass the original envelope downstream if all results have been processed successfully.
